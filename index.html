Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Our Commitment: FocusGuard was built to protect your attention, not harvest your data. We operate on a Local-First principle.

2. Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or the contents of your focus sessions to any external servers.

3. Local Storage: All data—including your "Intentions" and "History"—is stored exclusively on your local device using the chrome.storage.local API. If you delete the extension, this data is removed.

4. Permissions Usage:

  • Alarms: Used to trigger the end of your focus session.
  • Tabs & Scripting: Used only to display the "Session Complete" guard screen on your active tabs. We do not read your page content.

5. No Third Parties: FocusGuard has zero trackers, zero analytics, and zero advertisements. Your focus remains your own.
